About us

Our Story

“A cabinet of curiosities
that pours back.”

Funky Monkey SKG is a one-of-a-kind cocktail bar and bistro that opened in 2022 in the heart of Thessaloniki. The entire space is decorated with upcycled vintage objects — old suitcases, antique furniture, repurposed curiosities — creating a cozy, intimate atmosphere unlike anywhere else.

Family-run with roots in Kozani, ingredients are sourced partly from their own family farm. Enjoy DJ nights Thursday through Sunday featuring classic hits, afrohouse, and funk, alongside weekend and Friday brunch ceremonies. Live events are held regularly throughout the winter.
